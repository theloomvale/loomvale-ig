# Armillary Dome - Image 8

A grand astronomical chamber with towering arched windows showcasing constellations and planets. The center features a monumental brass armillary sphere with intricate concentric rings and celestial markers. Golden candelabras with dozens of candles illuminate the space on both sides, creating warm light that contrasts with the cool starlight from the domed ceiling. Star charts and globes are arranged on a central table, surrounded by ornate architectural details and zodiacs. The entire composition evokes a temple of cosmic knowledge.
